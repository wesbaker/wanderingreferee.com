Hi, my name is Wes Baker. I'm a tabletop gamer, miniature painter, and game master. This site is where I write about the games I'm running, the models I'm painting, the board games I'm playing, and the books I'm reading.

## What I'm running

Right now I'm deep into [Land of Eem](https://landofeem.com/) with my kids. We're two sessions in and we've saved Christmas and struck out across the Mucklands.

On the adult side, I've been playing in a Pathfinder 2e campaign at work for a while now. I play a Goblin Swashbuckler named Flick who has a flair for the dramatic and a hell of a mouth.

## What I'm painting

Currently working through some 3D printed [Guild Ball Lumberjacks](https://www.myminifactory.com/object/3d-print-guild-ball-the-lumberjacks-715055) as I try to get my son interested in playing Guild Ball. After that, I'm waiting on the [Starcraft Tabletop Miniatures Game](https://starcraft-tmg.com/) before I start assembling and painting those.
